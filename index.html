our heritage
A Legacy of Italian Soul Luna Rossa began as a small family kitchen in the heart of Rome, where recipes weren't written in books but passed down through hands that knew the exact texture of perfect pasta dough. Today, we carry that same spirit to your table. Every tomato is hand- picked, every truffle is sourced from the Umbrian hills, and every dish is a chapter of our family history, reimagined for those who appreciate the finer details of dining.
